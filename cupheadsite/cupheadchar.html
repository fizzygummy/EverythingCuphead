Personality: Cuphead is adventurous, impulsive, and thrill-seeking. Unlike his cautious brother, he is reckless and often jumps into danger without thinking.

Appearance: Cuphead has a red-and-white color scheme, with a large cup for a head, a short straw, and looks like he'd be called
"Rudolph The Red Nose Reindeer".

Abilities: Just like Mugman, Cuphead can shoot projectiles and use various super moves. He is the primary playable character in single-player
mode.

Role in Cuphead: Cuphead is the one who makes the fateful bet with The Devil, setting the events of the game into motion. Alongside Mugman, he fights to reclaim their souls.
