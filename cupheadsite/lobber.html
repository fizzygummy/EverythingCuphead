In-Game Description

"Medium range and good damage with a slower rate of fire."

Regular Attack

The Lobber

The Lobber fires explosive projectiles that arc in a parabola, making it ideal for hitting enemies above or below you.
It deals a lot of damage, but the shots move slowly and are harder to aim than traditional weapons.
The arc of the Lobber makes it more effective against flying enemies or bosses with multiple tiers.
While it has a slow rate of fire, the splash damage from each shot can clear out groups of enemies or deal heavy damage to bosses from a distance.

Super Attack

Kablooey

The Kablooey fires an incredibly large and powerful explosive projectile that deals massive damage upon impact.
It also creates a wide explosion radius, making it useful for clearing out multiple enemies or dealing huge damage to bosses.
However, it has a slow charging time, so you’ll need to find the right moment to unleash this devastating super attack.

Summary

The Lobber is great for defeating stationary bosses and focusing on dealing the most damage that you can.

Its arcing projectiles are perfect for enemies in the air or those that are difficult to hit with straight shots.
Against bosses like Grim Matchstick, the Lobber can help you land consistent hits while avoiding fire hazards and staying mobile.
However, the Lobber requires patience and timing, as it doesn’t have the rapid fire rate of other weapons.
