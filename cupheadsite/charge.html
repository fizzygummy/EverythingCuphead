Regular Attack

The Charge

The Charge is a unique weapon that requires you to charge up your shots before firing. The longer you hold the shot, the more powerful it becomes.
This weapon is perfect for taking out high-health bosses or large enemies that require concentrated firepower.
However, the Charge is slower to fire compared to rapid-fire weapons, so you’ll need to plan your timing and positioning carefully to make it effective.

Super Attack

The Radial Barrage

The Radial Barrage fires an incredibly powerful, fully-charged shot that does massive damage to anything in its path.
It’s great for quickly taking down tough enemies or bosses, but be mindful of the charging time, as it leaves you vulnerable while preparing the shot.

Summary

The Charge is a powerful weapon that rewards precise timing and planning. Its high damage output makes it ideal for difficult boss fights.

It’s especially useful against bosses with predictable patterns, allowing you to charge up a shot while avoiding attacks.
Against bosses like The Devil, the Charge allows you to land devastating blows as he pauses in between his attack phases.
